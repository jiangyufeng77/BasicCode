About this HIT:

  • Please only participate in this HIT if you have normal color vision.

  • It should take about 5 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving visual perception. You'll see a series of pairs of images. In each pair, images are generated using different algorithms. Your task is to determine which image is more realistic. Sometimes the images may look very plausible.

  • You will complete a short practice (less than 1 minute) before starting the main task.
